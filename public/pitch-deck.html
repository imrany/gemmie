Gemmie

Empowering Kenyan Students with Secure, AI-Driven Research Tools

An AI-powered research platform with gen AI, web realtime search, deep search with an editor designed for educational institutions seeking data sovereignty, security, and localized solutions

Making advanced AI research accessible, secure, and affordable for Kenyan learners

The Problem

Kenyan students and institutions face critical barriers to AI adoption

  • Data Privacy Crisis: Educational data flows through foreign AI platforms with no institutional control or oversight
  • Security Vulnerabilities: Student research and academic work exposed to data breaches, misuse, and unauthorized access
  • Lack of Localization: Global AI tools don't understand Kenyan context, curriculum, or regional educational needs
  • Unaffordable Solutions: Premium AI research tools price out most Kenyan students and under-resourced institutions
  • Limited Research Capabilities: Students lack access to powerful AI tools for deep research, analysis, and academic writing
  • No Institutional Control: Schools have no ability to manage, monitor, or customize AI tools for their students

Our Solution

Gemmie: A secure, locally-focused AI research platform

  • Complete Data Sovereignty: On-premise deployment gives institutions full control over student data and AI interactions
  • Enterprise-Grade Security: End-to-end encryption, access controls, and compliance with educational data protection standards
  • Kenyan-First Design: Localized for Kenyan curriculum, context, and educational requirements
  • Powerful Research Engine: Gen AI, web search, and deep search capabilities for comprehensive academic research
  • Intelligent Document Editor: Upload PDFs, edit, research within documents, generate summaries, and export in multiple formats
  • Flexible Deployment: Choose between on-premise hosting or secure cloud access based on institutional needs
  • Affordable Pricing: Student-friendly rates and institutional packages designed for Kenyan budgets
